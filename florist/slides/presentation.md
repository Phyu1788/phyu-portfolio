# Who's my person?
A local florist owner who takes orders by phone and text — losing customers to competitors with online shops.

---

# Their problem
No website means missed sales, manual errors, and customers who can't browse or buy 24/7.

---

# What I built
A modern React florist site with product catalog, shopping cart, and seamless checkout experience.

---

# How I built it
React components for reusable UI, CSS for styling, state management for cart, and modal for product details.

---

# Why it matters
Turns a phone-only business into a professional online storefront — more orders, happier customers, real growth.
